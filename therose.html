THE ROSE

INTRODUCTION

The Rose (Korean: 더 로즈) is a South Korean indie-rock band under their company Windfall and partnered up with Transparent Arts. The band is composed of four members: Kim Woo-sung (vocals, guitar), Park Do-joon (keyboard, vocals), Lee Ha-joon (drums), and Lee Jae-hyeong (bass).[1] Prior to its debut with J&Star, the band had long been a popular indie group before making its official mainstream release with "Sorry" on August 3, 2017.（SOURCE)
