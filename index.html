MEGAPORT! The Biggest Music Festival In TW!
Megaport is held near the Port of Kaohsiung. With its one-of-a-kind harbor landscape and local custom, Megaport has become a place that always brings back old memories for the locals. The festival brings well-known artists and experienced performers to its stages, inviting them to collaborate with artists or bands from the new generation, and further triggering chemical reactions among different generations. Under the theme of “life” and “music” of Megaport Festival, the audience gets to resonate the ups and downs in their life experience with a variety genres of music: unique indie music, powerful rock and hip-hop, enchanting pop songs, etc. Looking back on the past few years, Megaport had invited both local and foreign artists to its stages. Local artists, for examples, Sorry Youth, Nine one one, No Party for Cao Dong, Fire EX, Leo Wang, Sunset Rollercoaster, CHTHONIC, Amazing Show, Flesh Juicer, Accusefive, Jo-Hsuan Vivian Hsu, Crowd Lu, Sin-Je Angelica Lee, Sandee Chan, 9m88, Enno Cheng, etc. (Source)
